Background

Forged in the ancient arts of Telecommunications Engineering, this guardian has dedicated their existence to protecting digital realms from the forces of chaos. Their mastery of Identity & Access Management (IAM) allows them to safeguard the lifecycle of digital identities in this vast and perilous world.

Their magic extends through both the celestial Cloud realms and the earthly On-Premise fortresses, weaving enchantments of authentication, authorization, and identity governance under the most sacred protection standards.

Driven by an obsessive quest for automation, this guardian seeks to eliminate repetitive toil through the arcane power of scripting and orchestration, freeing mortals from mundane labor and allowing them to focus on more strategic endeavors. Every manual task discovered is seen as a challenge to be conquered.
